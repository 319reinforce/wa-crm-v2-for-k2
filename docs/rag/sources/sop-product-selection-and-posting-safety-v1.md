# Moras SOP: Product Selection And Posting Safety (v1)

## Scope

- Scene focus: `follow_up`, `gmv_inquiry`, `trial_intro`
- Goal: improve conversion and reduce spam/violation risk by standardizing posting rhythm and product selection logic.

## Product Selection Logic (External-facing)

Use this wording when creators ask "how products are selected":

```text
Moras uses TikTok real-time sales signals and trend data, then cross-checks those signals with your content profile.
The objective is to recommend products that both fit your page style and show proven conversion potential.
```

## Audience Fit Guidance

- Creators are not forced into one category.
- Recommend selecting products aligned with account niche.
- For wellness/personal-development audiences, prioritize:
  - wellness & self-care
  - lifestyle essentials
  - practical daily-use items
- Skip products that clearly conflict with audience trust.

## Posting Cadence SOP

- Preferred safe range: 3–5 posts/day.
- Hard recommendation: do not exceed 5/day for risk control.
- Spread posts across time slots:
  - morning
  - afternoon
  - evening

Why: avoid cluster posting that may trigger spam-like behavior and reduce each video's distribution chance.

## Qualified Video Rule

A video is counted as qualified if:

1. Product link is included.
2. Video stays live (not deleted/removed).

Operational note:
- Deleted/removed videos are not eligible for base pay.

## Missing Link Handling

If creator says link disappeared, explain:

- inventory went out of stock
- seller updated listing
- TikTok compliance re-check

Then advise creator to verify Product Showcase or contact seller/brand.

## Script Editing Request (Current Beta)

Standard response:

```text
At this beta stage, script generation is fully automated and manual script editing is not yet supported.
We are collecting feedback and evaluating this feature for future phases.
```

## Suggested Reply Template

```text
Great question! To keep your account safe and stable, I recommend 3–5 videos per day, spaced across different time slots.
For product choice, prioritize items that naturally fit your audience profile — this usually improves both trust and conversion.
If you want, I can help you review your next 3 picks before posting.
```
