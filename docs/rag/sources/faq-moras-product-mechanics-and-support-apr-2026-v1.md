# Moras FAQ: Product Mechanics And Creator Control (April 2026)

Source: `/Users/depp/Downloads/达人建联各SOP话述(4月版).docx`
Language policy: explain product logic clearly without overselling outcomes.

## Scope

- Scene focus: `first_contact`, `content_request`, `gmv_inquiry`, `general`, `video_not_loading`
- Goal: standardize how Moras is explained when creators ask how the system works, how products are chosen, and how much control they keep.

## How Moras Works

Use this structure when a creator asks what Moras actually does:

1. Smart product hunting using TikTok sales and trend signals
2. AI script + voiceover + rendering
3. One-click publishing support
4. Data-driven learning from account performance

Short explanation:

```text
Moras acts as an AI content engine for TikTok Shop creators.
It helps with product discovery, script creation, video generation, and publishing support so creators can test more shoppable content with less manual work.
```

## Product Recommendation Logic

- Moras uses TikTok real-time sales and trend data.
- The system cross-checks those signals with the creator profile or page style.
- The goal is to recommend items with both conversion potential and audience fit.
- The creator is not forced to promote a specific category.

## Audience Fit Guidance

- Creators should prioritize products that fit their niche and audience trust.
- For audiences around wellness, lifestyle, or practical daily use, recommend products that feel natural to those themes.
- Skip products that clearly conflict with the page identity.

## Creator Control

- Recommendations are suggestions, not forced publishing.
- The creator can review, approve, or skip products and videos.
- Nothing should be described as posting automatically without the creator's review.
- Videos are used on the creator's own account, and the creator controls whether they stay live.

## Similar Video / Safety Concern

- Moras should not be described as pushing the exact same finished video to every creator.
- Explain that generated outputs vary through different combinations of visuals, pacing, captions, or structure.
- Do not convert that explanation into a `zero risk` promise.

## Current Product Constraints

- iOS support exists now; Android support should not be implied unless confirmed separately.
- Manual script editing is not supported in the current beta flow.
- Spanish support is in progress; do not claim it is already live.
