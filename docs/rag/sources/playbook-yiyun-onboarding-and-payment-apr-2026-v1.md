# Yiyun Playbook: Onboarding And Payment Guardrails (April 2026)

Source: `/Users/depp/Downloads/达人建联各SOP话述(4月版).docx`
Language policy: keep creator-facing wording short, direct, and conservative.

## Scope

- Scene focus: `first_contact`, `monthly_inquiry`, `mcn_binding`, `video_not_loading`, `payment_issue`
- Operator: `Yiyun`
- Goal: keep Yiyun replies operationally consistent during onboarding, payment explanation, MCN hesitation, and setup/support flows.

## Core Style Rules

- Reply briefly and answer the direct question first.
- Keep to one question or one next step at a time.
- Prefer simple operational wording over long persuasion.
- Do not promise guaranteed earnings, guaranteed safety, or exact payout dates.
- Do not pressure creators into MCN binding or same-day decisions.

## Onboarding And Invite Code SOP

- If the creator is interested, guide them through:
  - download the app
  - log in
  - enter the invite code
- Ask for WhatsApp when faster setup or real-time support will help.
- If the creator prefers email-only communication, continue on email and ask for the Moras Username after signup.
- After the creator shares the username:
  - confirm priority support / backend follow-up
  - encourage the first post soon
  - avoid inflated sales or adoption claims

## Monthly Fee Explanation

- The standard explanation is `$20/month`.
- Preferred wording: the fee can be deducted from subsidy or eligible earnings when available.
- Do not imply forced upfront payment when the creator asks whether they must prepay.
- Keep settlement wording short and practical; describe it as a weekly settlement flow unless the creator explicitly asks for more detail.

## MCN Binding Explanation

- Explain MCN linking as the path to unlock fuller usage, tracking, and support workflows.
- Clarify what it does **not** mean:
  - Moras does not control the creator account
  - Moras does not post without the creator's action
  - do not imply a hidden long-term lock-in
- If the creator hesitates, stay calm and informative instead of argumentative.

## Setup Trouble / Video Not Loading

- Acknowledge the issue first.
- Confirm that tech follow-up or priority support is being arranged.
- Ask for Moras Username or screenshot only if needed.
- Keep the reply practical and avoid unrelated sales language.

## Payment Support

- Ask for payout details only when necessary to resolve settlement.
- If payout details are needed, request:
  - PayPal email
  - full name on the PayPal account
- Do not promise same-day manual settlement.

## Do

- Use WhatsApp as the preferred fast-support channel when appropriate.
- Accept email-only communication when the creator declines WhatsApp.
- Ask for Moras Username after signup so support can follow the account.
- Keep explanations short when the creator only asks one narrow question.

## Do Not

- Do not use scarcity campaign lines like `Top 100`, `Last 50 spots`, or other time-limited promo hooks in support replies.
- Do not use wording like `guaranteed`, `definitely`, `100% safe`, or `zero risk`.
- Do not over-explain internal payout mechanics when a short operational answer is enough.
- Do not attack other MCNs or pressure creators who are unsure about binding.

## Suggested Reply Template

```text
Thanks for checking.

The monthly fee is $20, and when eligible subsidy or earnings are available, it can be deducted from that instead of asking you to prepay first.

If you want, I can also help you with the next setup step here.
```
