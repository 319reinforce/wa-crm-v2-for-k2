# Moras SOP: Violation Appeal And Risk Control (v1)

## Scope

- Scene focus: `payment_issue`, `follow_up`, `gmv_inquiry`
- Goal: reduce creator account risk, provide compliant appeal guidance, standardize support wording.

## Core Principles

- Be transparent: never promise "zero risk" on TikTok.
- Prioritize prevention before compensation.
- Use consistent, evidence-based language in appeals.
- Avoid aggressive or blaming tone toward TikTok review team.

## Pre-Posting Risk Checklist

Before posting any AI-generated video, verify:

- Color Mismatch: the video color matches actual product.
- Shape Mismatch: product shape matches real item.
- Pattern/Design Inconsistency: logos/textures/patterns are not obviously wrong.
- Product link is valid and currently active in TikTok Shop.

If any mismatch exists, do not post.

## Creator Reply Templates

### A. Creator asks: "Will my account be 100% safe?"

```text
I want to be fully transparent with you: no one can guarantee a zero-risk experience on TikTok.
Even manual creators can get random flags.

Our goal is to reduce risk with pre-check logic and safer product selection, and we keep improving based on real creator feedback.
If you want, I can also help you review the next video before posting.
```

### B. Creator reports a violation

```text
I completely understand your concern — your account safety comes first.
From our side, we suggest doing an immediate appeal and checking whether the issue came from product title/description keywords.

I can send you a ready-to-use appeal template and a quick checklist for your next upload.
```

## Appeal Template (Reference)

```text
Dear TikTok Review Team,

I am writing to appeal the violation flag on my account regarding "False or Misleading Representations." I believe this video does not violate TikTok Shop policy.

This is an original video created with AI-assisted production and based on official seller listing information. Product attributes shown in the video reflect the seller-provided details to the best of my knowledge.

The AI was used for creative production only and not to fabricate product facts. I have attached relevant seller listing references for verification.

I respectfully request a manual review of this case. I am committed to creating authentic and policy-compliant TikTok Shop content.

Thank you for your time and consideration.
```

## Post-Violation Handling SOP

1. Acknowledge creator concern within 10 minutes.
2. Send appeal template + evidence list.
3. Ask creator to provide:
   - violation screenshot
   - product link
   - posted video link
4. Suggest temporary pause on similar product category until review returns.
5. Log case in internal tracker and monitor recurrence pattern.

## Compensation Reminder Wording

```text
Although compensation may apply, we still want to avoid any account risk.
Please re-check product consistency (color/shape/pattern) before the next post.
Safety first, then scale.
```
