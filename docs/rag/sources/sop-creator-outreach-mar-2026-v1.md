## 5) Product & Policy FAQ SOP

### Q: How does Moras generate videos / product logic?

```text
That’s a great question...
• Data-Driven Selection: AI syncs with TikTok data to analyze what is selling.
• Custom Scripting & Voiceover
• Unique Visual Rendering (reduce "unoriginal content" risk)
• Sales Optimization: focus on Product-to-Sales logic.
In short: You provide the Brand Identity; Moras provides the Content Engine.
```

### Q: Product recommendation logic?

```text
Great question! Moras uses TikTok real-time sales data and cross-references with your content style and audience profile.
Goal: recommend products that fit your page and have proven conversion.
```

### Q: Posting limits?

```text
To keep account safety and quality, we recommend no more than 5 videos/day.
A practical sweet spot is 3–5 posts/day, and spread them across different time slots (morning/afternoon/evening).
```

### Q: Missing product link?

```text
Possible reasons:
• Inventory issues
• Seller listing updates
• TikTok compliance review
This can be outside Moras control.
```

### Q: Can I edit scripts manually?

```text
Current beta is fully automated and does not support manual script editing yet.
Feature is under consideration in future phases.
```

### Q: How do I know a video is qualified?

```text
Two conditions:
1) includes product link
2) stays live (not deleted/removed)
```

### Q: Spanish support?

```text
Team is working on Spanish support.
Current app is English-only.
We can guide creators in English meanwhile and notify first once Spanish is live.
```

## 6) Violation & Appeal SOP

### Risk-prevention reminder before posting

```text
Please check product consistency before posting:
• Color Mismatch
• Shape Mismatch
• Pattern/Design Inconsistency
If any mismatch exists, do not post.
```

### Violation response (creator reassurance)

```text
To be transparent: no one can guarantee zero-risk on TikTok.
We are continuously optimizing and minimizing risk with pre-check + policy improvements.
```

### Appeal template (reference)

```text
Dear TikTok Review Team,
I am writing to appeal the violation flag on my account regarding 'False or Misleading Representations.' I believe this video does not violate TikTok Shop Policy.

This is an original video created using AI-assisted technology, combined with official reference materials and product descriptions sourced directly from the seller’s listing...

I respectfully ask for a manual review of this video. I am committed to creating high-quality, authentic shop content and would appreciate your reconsideration.

Thank you for your time.
```
